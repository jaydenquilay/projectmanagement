Our Story

Where It All Started

Velvet Room began with a simple vision: to bring the heart of Italian hospitality to the center of Vancouver. Founded by a family of culinary artisans, our story is woven with Sunday dinners, heirloom recipes, and a love for creating unforgettable dining experiences. What started as a cozy trattoria has now grown into a refined destination for those who crave soulful dishes and warm, moody elegance.

Meet Our Chef

Our Food, Our Values

At the helm of Velvet Room’s kitchen is Executive Chef Luca Romano, a visionary rooted in tradition. With a reverence for slow cooking and regional ingredients, Chef Luca brings you handcrafted pastas, seasonal antipasti, and desserts made with love. Every dish reflects our values: authenticity, quality, and the joy of sharing a meal with the ones you love.
